Influence & Legacy · Module 06

A Philosophy
That Persists

Six channels broadcasting from one source. Each module below is a domain where Rams's restraint is still doing measurable work — half a century after the principles.

braun audio · DR Legacy Programme · Six Channels
CH·01

Apple & Jonathan Ive

Jonathan Ive, Apple's longtime Chief Design Officer, repeatedly cited Rams as his primary influence. The original iMac, iPod, and iPhone all bear direct formal relationships to Braun products of the 1960s and 70s.

Industrial Lineage
10/10 · LIVE
CH·02

Museum Collections

Works by Rams are held in the permanent collections of MoMA New York, the Victoria & Albert Museum, the Design Museum London, and the Vitra Design Museum — among the most-collected industrial designers in history.

Permanent Collection
9/10 · LIVE
CH·03

Rams · 2018 Film

Director Gary Hustwit's documentary brought his philosophy to a new generation, exploring not only the design legacy but his deep concern for overconsumption and environmental sustainability.

Cultural Broadcast
7/10 · LIVE
CH·04

Education & Curriculum

The Ten Principles are taught in design schools globally and have become the closest thing industrial design has to a universal ethical framework — a generation of product, UX, and interaction designers educated through these ten ideas.

Teaching Standard
9/10 · LIVE
CH·05

Sustainability Advocacy

Long before sustainability became a design industry keyword, Rams argued that good design is inherently ecological — built to last, repaired, passed on. Principle 9 anticipated the circular economy by decades.

Circular · Principle 9
10/10 · LIVE
CH·06

Vitsœ Today

The 606 Universal Shelving System and 620 Chair Programme remain in continuous production, manufactured in exactly the form Rams designed them. "Living better, with less, that lasts longer" — direct expression of his ethos, still relevant sixty years on.

In Production
10/10 · LIVE
